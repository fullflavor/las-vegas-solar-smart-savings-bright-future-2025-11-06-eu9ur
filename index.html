Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about solar installation, financing, tax credits, and more.

How much can I save with solar energy?

The amount you save depends on several factors including your current electricity consumption, roof size and orientation, local electricity rates, and available sunlight in your area. On average, Las Vegas homeowners save between $10,000 to $30,000 over the first 25 years of system operation. Many customers see a 50-90% reduction in their monthly electricity bills. Additionally, with the federal Investment Tax Credit covering 30% of installation costs, combined with Nevada state incentives and utility rebates, your total savings can exceed $15,000 in the first year alone. Our expert team can provide a detailed analysis and personalized savings estimate for your specific home during a free consultation.

What are the available financing options?

We offer multiple financing options to suit every budget and financial situation. Our zero-down payment solar loans allow you to go solar without any upfront costs, and you start saving immediately on your electricity bills. We also offer low-interest financing with flexible terms ranging from 5 to 25 years. For those who prefer not to own the system outright, we provide solar lease options with minimal monthly payments and no maintenance responsibilities. Additionally, Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) allow you to pay only for the electricity your system produces, often at rates lower than your current utility company charges. Each option has distinct advantages, and our financial advisors can help you determine which is best for your situation.

How long does the installation process take?

The entire process from initial consultation to system activation typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on permitting requirements and your local utility company's approval timeline. The actual physical installation on your roof usually takes just 1-3 days, depending on the size of your system. Before installation begins, our team handles all the paperwork, including permit applications and financing arrangements. After installation, we conduct final inspections and testing to ensure everything is operating at peak efficiency. Your system will be connected to the grid and actively generating clean energy within days of installation completion. Our streamlined process minimizes disruption to your home and gets you saving money as quickly as possible.

What about the 30% federal tax credit? How does it work?

The federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC) is a significant incentive that allows you to claim 30% of your solar installation costs as a tax credit on your federal income tax return. For example, if your system costs $25,000, you can claim a $7,500 tax credit, reducing your federal income tax liability by that amount. This credit is available for both purchased systems and financed systems. The credit has been extended through 2032, with the percentage gradually decreasing in subsequent years. To qualify, you must have sufficient federal tax liability to claim the full credit. Our team can help you understand how this credit applies to your specific situation and ensure you claim the maximum benefit. Many customers find that this credit, combined with state incentives and utility rebates, covers a significant portion of their installation costs.

What happens to my solar system during power outages?

Standard grid-tied solar systems automatically shut down during power outages as a safety measure to protect utility workers. However, if you add a battery storage system like the Tesla Powerwall or LG Chem batteries to your setup, you can store excess solar energy and use it during outages, providing backup power for essential appliances and systems. These battery systems can be configured to power specific circuits in your home, keeping lights, refrigeration, medical equipment, and other critical systems running during grid outages. Battery storage systems have become increasingly affordable and can be added to your solar installation at the time of setup or retrofitted later. Our team can discuss battery options and help you determine if backup power is right for your home and budget.
