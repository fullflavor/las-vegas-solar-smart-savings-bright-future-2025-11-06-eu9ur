Information We Collect

Personal Information Provided by You

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us when you request a quote, schedule a consultation, sign up for our services, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Mailing address and property information
  • Current electricity bills and energy usage data
  • Financial information for financing purposes
  • Tax identification numbers and income information
  • Communication preferences and feedback

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type, operating system, and device information
  • Pages visited, links clicked, and time spent on pages
  • Referrer source and search keywords
  • Geographic location data
  • Interaction with website features and tools

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, including credit reporting agencies, financing partners, utility companies, and public records. This information helps us verify your identity, assess your eligibility for financing, and provide accurate quotes and recommendations.

How We Use Your Information

Las Vegas Solar uses the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Service Delivery: To provide quotes, schedule consultations, process orders, and deliver solar installation services
  • Financing: To assess financing eligibility, process applications, and manage loan or lease agreements
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, send service updates, and provide customer support
  • Marketing: To send promotional materials, newsletters, and information about special offers (with your consent)
  • Analytics: To analyze website usage, improve our services, and enhance user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security, and technical issues
  • Business Operations: To operate our business, improve our services, and develop new offerings

Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With contractors, installers, financing partners, and other service providers necessary to deliver our services
  • Financial Institutions: With banks, credit unions, and financing companies to process loans and payment arrangements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets
  • Affiliates: With our parent company, subsidiaries, and affiliated entities
  • Marketing Partners: With third parties for marketing and promotional purposes (only with your consent)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you personally.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, improve your browsing experience, and analyze website traffic. We use both session-based and persistent cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver targeted advertising
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

Third-Party Tracking

We use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and other analytics tools to track and analyze website usage. These services may set their own cookies on your device. You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings or opt-out tools provided by these services.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect website functionality and your ability to use certain features of our services.

Data Security

Las Vegas Solar takes data security seriously and implements appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to secure data transmission over the internet.

However, no security system is impenetrable. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and account information. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.
